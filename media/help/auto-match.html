Auto-accept alignment results

When aligning to Wikidata, an intital query ('pass0') finds records that share one or more authority identifiers with your record, for example if your record already includes a link/match to GeoNames id 987654, and a record in Wikidata has also been linked to GeoNames id 987654.

You can click auto-accept here, to accept all pass0 matches, OR review them and manually make match decisions, as you would with pass1 and pass2 candidate 'hits'.
