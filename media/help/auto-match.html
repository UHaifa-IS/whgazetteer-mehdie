Auto-accept "pass 0" alignment results

When aligning to Wikidata or the WHG index, the initial query ('pass 0') finds records that share one or more authority identifiers with your record, for example if your record already includes a link/match to GeoNames id 987654, and a record in Wikidata or the WHG index has also been linked to GeoNames id 987654.

You can click auto-accept here, to accept all pass 0 matches, OR review them and manually make match decisions, as you would with pass 1 or pass 2 candidate 'hits'.

For Wikidata: Your place records in the WHG database will be augmented with geometry and/or concordance links from the matched Wikidata record.

For Accessioning: Your place records will be indexed, linked with existing records that share one or more of their authority identifiers -- each joining a cluster of one or more attestations for a given place.
