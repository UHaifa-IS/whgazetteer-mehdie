Geographic Bounds

By selecting either a pre-defined region or user-defined Study Area from these dropdown lists, you can filter potential candidate matches for this reconciliation task, ensuring that all lie within those spatial extents.

You can create an appropriate study area if you don't already have one. You will be returned to this screen and it will be an option in that dropdown list.

Note that any geography supplied in your records (geographic coordinates, or country codes), will take precedence in filtering and/or ranking candidate "hits."
